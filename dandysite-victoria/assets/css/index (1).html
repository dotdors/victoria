Sarah Stogner — D.A., 143rd Judicial District of Texas

Sarah Stogner is District Attorney of Texas' 143rd Judicial District. She previously spent years in commercial litigation, which included providing legal counsel for energy-related entities.

After moving to West Texas, she became a passionate advocate for plugging orphaned oil wells — and, soon, a fighter against governmental corruption and incompetence.

She was asked to run for District Attorney in 2024 and won, taking office in 2025.

"An Unlikely Prosecutor Is Now the Law in Texas Oil Country."

— The New York Times
